Our Journey

The team behind Stevie has spun out of the Robotics And Innovation Lab whose primary interests are Human Robot Interaction, actuation, computer hardware, dexterous manipulation, mechanical design, low-cost solutions and low-level control. By leveraging peer reviewed literature published on these topics, the technology being developed stems from years of fundamental research into robotics.
